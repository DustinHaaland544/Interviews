- Why do you want to leave your current job?
Ans: I want to leave my job because I am seeking a fresh challenge with an exciting and innovative
such as yours that has ambitious plans for the future.

- Why should we hire you?
Ans: I believe you should hire me because I am a very fast learner and I need little supervision moving 
forward. I also belive you should hire me because I will represent your business positively, and I will
treat my co-workers and clients with respect and patience. I will also ensure to find ways to help the 
business grow, whilst also helping you save money by using resources in a frugal and responsible manner.

- What did you dislike about your previous job?
Ans: There was only one thing that fraustrated me slightly about my last job, and that was the fact that 
I felt we could have achieved so much more as a company. There were lots of opportunities in the market
for us to expand and grow, but the company owners seemed satisfied with where the company was at.
