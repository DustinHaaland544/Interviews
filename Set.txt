Interview Preparation Questions:

1. Tell me about yourself.

2. Tell me about your experience with this type of work.

3. Why do you want to work here?

4. Why did you leave your last job?

5. What are your greatest strengths?

6. What are your weaknesses/challenges?

7. Tell me about a time when you had to work under pressure or tight deadlines?

8. How do you handle a situation in which you aren't sure how to help a customer?

9. Tell me about your greatest accomplishment/achievement?

10. Tell me about a time when you had conflicts of interest with a colleague and how did you resolve it?

11. Tell me about a stressful work situation you experienced and how did you overcome it?

12. Are there tools that you use to help you stay organized?

13. What is your salary expectation?

14. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

15. Why should we hire you?

16. Tell me about a time when you made a mistake.

17. How would you handle an angry/irritated customer/client?

18. What do you know about our company? 

19. How do you handle stress at workplace?

20. Tell me how you manage disagreements with your manager? / Tell me about a time you disagreed with your manager and how did you handle the situation?

21. What do you think success would look like in this role?

22. What do you know about our company? / What do you know about us?

23. What do you know about this job/role that you have applied for?

24. When were you most satisfied in your last job?

25. What did you least like about your last job?

26. What is your greatest failure, and what did you learn from it?

27. What was the most difficult period in your life and how did you deal with it?

28. What assignment was too difficult for you, and how did you resolve the issue?

29. Do you have any question for me/us?

30. Describe how you would handle a situation if you were required to finish multiple tasks by the end of the day, and there was no conceivable way that you
    could finish them.
